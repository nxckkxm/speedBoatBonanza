Frequently Asked Questions

How to play Speed Boat Bonanza?

Speed Boat Bonanza is an exciting racing game where you navigate your boat through various obstacles and compete against others. Tap either side of the screen to move the boat towards that side.

How to save my game progress?

Your game progress is automatically saved to your device.

Troubleshooting

Why is the game not loading?

The game doesn't need an internet connection to work so try restarting the app. If the problem persists, contact support.

How to report a bug?

You can report bugs via the contact form above. Please provide detailed information about the issue.
